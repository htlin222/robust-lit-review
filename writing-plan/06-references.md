1. Schulhoff et al. "The Prompt Report: A Systematic Survey of Prompting Techniques." arXiv 2406.06608, 2024.

2. Sahoo et al. "A Systematic Survey of Prompt Engineering in Large Language Models." arXiv 2402.07927, 2024.
   — 29 categorized prompt engineering techniques

3. Shinn et al. "Reflexion: Language Agents with Verbal Reinforcement Learning." NeurIPS 2023.

4. Zheng et al. "Judging LLM-as-a-Judge." NeurIPS 2024.
   — LLM-as-judge evaluation methodology
